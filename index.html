About

At Explore Ireland We organise and coordinate various trips on a regular basis, from short drives on scenic routes, to camping trips, car shows and 4x4 off-road tours!

Please have a look at the brief descriptions below, and if you'd like to discuss in more detail, please CONTACT US now or join our community FACEBOOK GROUP for regular updates.

Trip Options

Day Drives

We organise regular full and half day tours of various scenic routes, predominantly on the West of Ireland's Wild Atlantic Way.

All vehicle types can participate, as we stick to tarred roads, and families are welcome!

Regular stops are included for coffee/tea and lunch breaks could be a quaint restaurant or a picnic on the beach!

Camping Trips

From a night away, to long weekend camping excursions, we love getting out into nature!

You don't need an expensive camping setup to take part. Just bring a tent, food and drinks and you're on your way to a fantastic time-out from day to day stresses.

Some folk use portable gas cookers and others prefer a BBQ. Before reaching our campsite we'll stop for supplies, such as firelighters, coal, snacks etc.

4x4 Adventures

Join one of our exhilarating 4x4 routes which takes us exploring green-lanes, forests and beaches, but fully off-road.

All experience-levels are catered for, but you must have a 4x4 with a relatively decent ground-clearance.

We always have experienced mechanics and recovery vehicles on the trips for your peace of mind.

Special Events

Explore Ireland also takes part in various car shows in Ireland.

These are really relaxed days just to get out, be social and bring the family for a day of fun activities, car-boot sales and music.

We also take part in Ireland's famous Vintage car shows, so if your vehicle is a classic (over 20 y/o), please contact us to register so we can book a space for you.
